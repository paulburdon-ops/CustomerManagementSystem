# BusinessAI Platform

## Purpose

BusinessAI is a modular AI platform designed to help organisations work with their internal knowledge.

Primary objectives

- Local AI for confidential information
- Cloud AI for public information
- Modular integrations
- Provider-independent architecture
- Source-backed AI responses

---

## Architecture Principles

1. Business logic never talks directly to AI providers.

2. AI providers are replaceable.

3. Customer knowledge remains isolated.

4. Every AI response should be traceable to its source.

5. Security and privacy are default behaviours.
